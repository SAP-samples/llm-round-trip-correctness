The following description is about the final grades submission process. It begins when you unmute all gradebook columns. When unmuting all gradebook columns is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: preparing grades for SSO and importing gradebook. When importing gradebook is completed, the process is split into 3 parallel branches: selecting exam assignment, calculating the grades and applying special grades to individuals. Once calculating the grades occurs, you need to apply score criteria. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then reviewing grades summary needs to be done. Once reviewing grades summary occurs, you need to export the final grades. Once exporting the final grades occurs, submitting results to SSO needs to be done. The process is now completed.
