The following text is about the purchase order workflow. It starts with recording the basic details. After recording the basic details, you need to get approval from finance team. Once getting approval from finance team occurs, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: creating the purchase order and informing the supplier the order is ready for approval. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then getting approval from supplier needs to be done. Once getting approval from supplier occurs, you need to confirm the delivery date. The process is now completed.
