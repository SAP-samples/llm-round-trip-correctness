The following description is about the budget preparation process. It begins when you record basic details. When recording basic details is completed, you need to review the most recent budget. When reviewing the most recent budget is completed, one or more of the following paths should be executed: prioritizing three to five goals, defining three to five goals, determining ways of achieving the goals. After that, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: estimating the average monthly cost and calculating the total annual budget. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then you need to list all sources of income. After listing all sources of income, finalizing the budget should be done. Once finalizing the budget occurs, obtaining approval from top management should be done. The process is now completed.
