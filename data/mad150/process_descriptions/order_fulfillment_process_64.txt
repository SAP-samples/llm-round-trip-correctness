The following text is about the order fulfillment process. It begins when you record order details. When recording order details is completed, the next tasks should be performing quality checks and recording which products were taken from storage, they should be done in parallel. Once performing quality checks occurs, the next tasks should be checking that the products are in stock and requesting the ordered products from your inventory, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: printing packing slip and printing invoice. Once printing packing slip occurs, the next tasks should be getting approval, packaging the items securely and measuring products, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then one or more of the following paths should be executed: loading items into the delivery vehicle, arranging the delivery, examining your other orders for delivery overlaps. After that, sending a confirmation email to the customer should be done. The process is now completed.
