The following description is about the order fulfillment process. It starts with confirming that payment has been received. Once confirming that payment has been received occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: performing quality checks, checking that the products are in stock, recording which products were taken from storage. Once checking that the products are in stock occurs, requesting the ordered products from your inventory needs to be done. Then, printing packing slip needs to be done. When printing packing slip is completed, the next tasks should be examining your other orders for delivery overlaps and loading items into the delivery vehicle, they should be done in parallel. When examining your other orders for delivery overlaps is completed, you need to arrange the delivery. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then sending a confirmation email to the customer should be done. The process is now completed.
