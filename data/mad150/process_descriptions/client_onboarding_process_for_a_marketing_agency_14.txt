The following text is about the client onboarding process for a marketing agency. It starts with sending out a new client questionnaire. When sending out a new client questionnaire is completed, researching the campaign ideas needs to be done. After researching the campaign ideas, one or more of the following paths should be executed: informing client of project start, assigning work to the team. Afterwards, having a follow-up call needs to be done. The process is now completed.
