The following description is about the order fulfillment process. It begins when you record order details. After recording order details, the next tasks should be checking that the products are in stock and recording which products were taken from storage, they should be done in parallel. After checking that the products are in stock, requesting the ordered products from your inventory needs to be done. When recording which products were taken from storage is completed, you need to perform quality checks. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then one or more of the following paths should be executed: packaging the items securely, getting approval. When getting approval is completed, the next tasks should be printing packing slip, measuring products and attaching the shipping label to the packaging, they should be done in parallel. When attaching the shipping label to the packaging is completed, printing invoice should be done. After that, loading items into the delivery vehicle should be done. Once loading items into the delivery vehicle occurs, sending a confirmation email to the customer should be done. The process is now completed.
