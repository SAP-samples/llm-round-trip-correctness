The following description is about the accounts receivable process. It starts with recording the buyer's details. After recording the buyer's details, one or more of the following paths should be executed: sending the buyer your terms of sale, sending a credit application form to the buyer. Once sending the buyer your terms of sale occurs, you need to get approval for post-credit check review. When sending a credit application form to the buyer is completed, you need to run a credit check on the purchasing company. Then, one or more of the following paths should be executed: checking customer payment terms, generating the invoice. After checking customer payment terms, sending the invoice in your accounting software needs to be done. After that, making a second physical copy needs to be done. After making a second physical copy, recording the sales on credit of services should be done. The process is now completed.
