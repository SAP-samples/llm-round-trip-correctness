The text below is about the startup due diligence for a venture capitalist. It begins when you learn why startups fail so often. Once learning why startups fail so often occurs, you need to consider product necessity. After considering product necessity, you need to review relationship to founder. When reviewing relationship to founder is completed, you need to check income statements. After checking income statements, analyzing the reputation of competition should be done. When analyzing the reputation of competition is completed, checking patents should be done. The process is now completed.
