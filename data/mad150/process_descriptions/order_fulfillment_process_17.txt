The following text is about the order fulfillment process. It starts with confirming that payment has been received. Once confirming that payment has been received occurs, requesting the ordered products from your inventory needs to be done. Once requesting the ordered products from your inventory occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: getting approval, measuring products. When getting approval is completed, one or more of the following paths should be executed: attaching the shipping label to the packaging, packaging the items securely. Afterwards, one or more of the following paths should be executed: examining your other orders for delivery overlaps, arranging the delivery. After that, you need to send a confirmation email to the customer. The process is now completed.
