The following description is about the account payable process. It starts with filing a copy for internal records. When filing a copy for internal records is completed, you need to create a receiving report. After creating a receiving report, one or more of the following paths should be executed: reporting errors to relevant team member, confirming all documents have been amended. Afterwards, the next tasks should be entering the invoice into the accounts payable account and storing in paid invoice file, they should be done in parallel. The process is now completed.
