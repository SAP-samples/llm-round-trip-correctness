The following description is about the startup due diligence for a venture capitalist. It begins when you learn how to identify future failures. Once learning how to identify future failures occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: specificking product market share, making plans for future products. Afterwards, the next tasks should be reviewing number of employees and reviewing the business plan, they should be done in parallel. Once reviewing the business plan occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: reviewing enthusiasm, reviewing team chemistry, reviewing expertise. When reviewing enthusiasm is completed, the next tasks should be reviewing employee salary and benefits, reviewing company mission and direction and reviewing ability as a team leader, they should be done in parallel. When reviewing ability as a team leader is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: reviewing relationship to founder and reviewing trustworthiness. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then one or more of the following paths should be executed: checking cash flow, checking exit strategy. After checking cash flow, checking current debt needs to be done. After checking exit strategy, checking income statements needs to be done. Then, you need to analyze the reputation of competition. When analyzing the reputation of competition is completed, you need to check insurance. The process is now completed.
