The following text is about the client onboarding process for a marketing agency. It starts with having an onboarding call with the client. Once having an onboarding call with the client occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: researching the campaign ideas, updating the project management profile. After researching the campaign ideas, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: scheduling future calls and confirming questionnaire is complete. Afterwards, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: informing client of project start and assigning work to the team. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: having a follow-up call and making changes based on follow-up call. The process is now completed.
