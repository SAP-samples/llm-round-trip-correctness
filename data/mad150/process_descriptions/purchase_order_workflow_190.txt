The following description is about the purchase order workflow. It begins when you record the basic details. Once recording the basic details occurs, getting approval from finance team should be done. Once getting approval from finance team occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: creating the purchase order, informing the supplier the order is ready for approval. After that, getting approval from supplier needs to be done. When getting approval from supplier is completed, one or more of the following paths should be executed: recording the purchase order, sending details to the department, confirming the delivery date. Once recording the purchase order occurs, filing the purchase order should be done. The process is now completed.
