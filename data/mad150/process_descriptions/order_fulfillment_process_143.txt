The following text is about the order fulfillment process. It starts with confirming that payment has been received. Once confirming that payment has been received occurs, you need to request the ordered products from your inventory. Once requesting the ordered products from your inventory occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: getting approval, attaching the shipping label to the packaging. When getting approval is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: measuring products and packaging the items securely. After measuring products, one or more of the following paths should be executed: printing invoice, printing packing slip. Afterwards, one or more of the following paths should be executed: loading items into the delivery vehicle, arranging the delivery, examining your other orders for delivery overlaps. Afterwards, you need to send a confirmation email to the customer. The process is now completed.
