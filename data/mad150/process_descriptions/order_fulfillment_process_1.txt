The text below is about the order fulfillment process. It starts with recording order details. Once recording order details occurs, checking that the products are in stock should be done. After checking that the products are in stock, packaging the items securely needs to be done. When packaging the items securely is completed, you need to load items into the delivery vehicle. Once loading items into the delivery vehicle occurs, you need to send a confirmation email to the customer. The process is now completed.
