The following text is about the budget preparation process. It begins when you record basic details. When recording basic details is completed, reviewing the most recent budget should be done. When reviewing the most recent budget is completed, you need to prioritize three to five goals. After prioritizing three to five goals, you need to estimate the average monthly cost. Once estimating the average monthly cost occurs, you need to project total annual income. Once projecting total annual income occurs, you need to finalize the budget. After finalizing the budget, one or more of the following paths should be executed: requesting approval from top management, assigning management responsibilities. The process is now completed.
