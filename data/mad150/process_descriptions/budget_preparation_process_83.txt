The following description is about the budget preparation process. It begins when you record basic details. Once recording basic details occurs, reviewing the most recent budget should be done. After reviewing the most recent budget, you need to prioritize three to five goals. When prioritizing three to five goals is completed, calculating the total annual budget should be done. Once calculating the total annual budget occurs, projecting total annual income needs to be done. Once projecting total annual income occurs, recording financial data in a budget spreadsheet needs to be done. When recording financial data in a budget spreadsheet is completed, you need to request approval from top management. The process is now completed.
