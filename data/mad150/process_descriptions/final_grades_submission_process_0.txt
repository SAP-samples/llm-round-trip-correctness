The following description is about the final grades submission process. It begins when you ensure 'EX' or zero marks are applied as need. Once ensuring 'EX' or zero marks are applied as need occurs, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: selecting exam assignment and calculating the grades. Once selecting exam assignment occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: applying score criteria, applying special grades to individuals, preparing grades for SSO. When applying special grades to individuals is completed, importing gradebook should be done. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then one or more of the following paths should be executed: reviewing grades summary, applying any changes. When reviewing grades summary is completed, reviewing course summary needs to be done. Afterwards, you need to export the final grades. After exporting the final grades, the next tasks should be sending examiners grade approval sheet and submitting results to SSO, they should be done in parallel. The process is now completed.
