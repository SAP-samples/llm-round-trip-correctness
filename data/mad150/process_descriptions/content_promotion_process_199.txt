The following description is about the content promotion process. It starts with preparing a summary of your content. When preparing a summary of your content is completed, one or more of the following paths should be executed: sharing on social media, commenting on related blog posts. After commenting on related blog posts, one or more of the following paths should be executed: posting on related forums and groups, deep-linking to the content from older posts. Once posting on related forums and groups occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: performing outreach, repurposing your content. When repurposing your content is completed, one or more of the following paths should be executed: posting on Q&A sites, paying for promotion. The process is now completed.
