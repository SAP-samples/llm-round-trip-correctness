The annual meeting at SoKnow begins when the annual meeting is upcoming.

First, the management sends invitations to the employees.

The management leads the annual planning meeting, while the employees participate in the meeting.

The management conducts a review and discussion of the current strategy.

Subsequently, the strategy is reformulated, while the employees align for the strategy.

After the alignment and reformulation of the strategy, the reformulated strategy is communicated and then pursued.

Finally, the process ends with the annual meeting being upcoming.
