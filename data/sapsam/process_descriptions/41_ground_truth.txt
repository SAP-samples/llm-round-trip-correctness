The viticulture process in outdoor operations begins with the technical Jane Doe when a task arises.

First, the working time effort is estimated.

Next, the availability of resources is checked.

Then, a field employee is selected and contacted.

It is checked again if the employee is reachable.

If the employee is not reachable, the task is not assigned, and the availability of resources is checked again.

If the employee is reachable, the task is assigned to the employee, and the procedure for task completion is specified.

The field employee receives the task and confirms it.

Afterward, the field employee processes the task and completes it.

Subsequently, a completion report is made.

The completion report is documented by the technical Jane Doe, and the task is processed.
