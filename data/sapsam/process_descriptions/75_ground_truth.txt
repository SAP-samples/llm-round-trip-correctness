The process of viticulture in outdoor operations begins when a field employee receives the task "Grape harvest."

Next, information about the field location is received.

Then, the tools are loaded.

After that, the employee drives to the field.

There, the tools are unloaded.

Subsequently, the grape harvest is conducted by hand.

After the grape harvest, the tools are loaded again.

Then, the employee drives to the winery.

There, the tools are unloaded again.

Finally, the task is completed.
