The SoKnow process begins with the formulation of the consulting service by the Consulting team.
The Consulting team then analyzes the results of the workshops.
Following this, new strategic opportunities are developed.
A report with the results and objectives is then formulated.
This report is forwarded to the Management team.
The Management team reads the report and checks if it is in order.
If the report is not in order, it is sent back for correction and then rechecked.
If the report is in order, it is immediately forwarded.
Finally, the consulting service is formulated, and the process ends with the formulation of the consulting service.
