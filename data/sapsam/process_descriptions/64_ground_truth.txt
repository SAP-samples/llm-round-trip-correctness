The process begins with the creation of an application.

Once the application is created, it is received by the loan provider.

The document is then controlled to ensure all necessary information is present.

An exclusive decision is made based on the completeness of the document.

If the document is complete, it is accepted, and the process concludes with the completion of providing.

If the document is incomplete, it is returned to the applicant to complete the missing information.

After the applicant completes the missing information, the document is admitted, and the process finishes with the providing.
