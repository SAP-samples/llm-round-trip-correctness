The application process begins with the opening of the application process by a student.

The student conducts the application.

Then, the Department of Admission and Enrollment accepts the applications and provides the applicant files.

The WI Selection Committee checks the eligibility of the applicants.

Subsequently, the Department of Admission and Enrollment evaluates the results of the admission test and selects the applicants who will be admitted.

The student accepts the admission.

After that, the Department of Admission and Enrollment enrolls the students and sends the enrollment documents.

Finally, the application process is completed.
