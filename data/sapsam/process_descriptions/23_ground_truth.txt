
The process begins when the patient enters short-term treatment in the designated treatment chambers, initiated by the doctor.

The first step is to evaluate the condition of the patient.

After the evaluation, the patient is sent to the laboratory for testing performed by the technicans.

Once the laboratory tests are completed, the patient is treated by the doctor based on the test results.

Following the treatment, the doctor prescribes the necessary medicines.

The process concludes when the prescription is delivered to the patient.
