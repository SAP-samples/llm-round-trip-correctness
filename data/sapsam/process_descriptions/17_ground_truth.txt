The process begins when an email account is needed in der Email Account Creation Portal.

The first step is to log on to the email provider's website and then chooses a package.

The system checks if a personal account is available.

If it is not available, the process ends with the private account being unavailable.

If a personal account is available, the private account welcome page and provides personal information is entered.

Next, personal information has to be provided. For this, both the full names have to be entered and a photo ID must be uploaded.

Once the full names are provided and the photo ID is uploaded, the personal data is confirmed, and new email ID and password are generated.

The process concludes with the creation of the private email.
