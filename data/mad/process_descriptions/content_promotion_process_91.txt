The following text is about the content promotion process. It starts with preparing a summary of your content. After preparing a summary of your content, one or more of the following paths should be executed: posting on Q&A sites, deep-linking to the content from older posts. When posting on Q&A sites is completed, submitting to link roundups should be done. Once deep-linking to the content from older posts occurs, the next tasks should be emailing your subscribers and paying for promotion, they should be done in parallel. The process is now completed.
