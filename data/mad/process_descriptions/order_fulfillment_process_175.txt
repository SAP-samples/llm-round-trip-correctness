The following description is about the order fulfillment process. It starts with recording order details. When recording order details is completed, the next tasks should be performing quality checks and requesting the ordered products from your inventory, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the next tasks should be packaging the items securely, measuring products and getting approval, they should be done in parallel. When measuring products is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: attaching the shipping label to the packaging and printing packing slip. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then loading items into the delivery vehicle needs to be done. After loading items into the delivery vehicle, you need to send a confirmation email to the customer. The process is now completed.
