The text below is about the startup due diligence for a venture capitalist. It starts with learning how to identify future failures. After learning how to identify future failures, checking location of startup needs to be done. Once checking location of startup occurs, the next tasks should be reviewing relationship to founder, reviewing expertise and reviewing ability as a team leader, they should be done in parallel. When reviewing relationship to founder is completed, reviewing employee salary and benefits needs to be done. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then checking quarterly projections should be done. When checking quarterly projections is completed, projecting the market share should be done. After projecting the market share, checking past lawsuits needs to be done. The process is now completed.
