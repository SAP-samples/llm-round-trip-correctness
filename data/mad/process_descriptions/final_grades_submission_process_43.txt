The following description is about the final grades submission process. It begins when you ensure 'EX' or zero marks are applied as need. Once ensuring 'EX' or zero marks are applied as need occurs, selecting exam assignment needs to be done. After selecting exam assignment, the next tasks should be reviewing course summary, applying any changes and reviewing grades summary, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the next tasks should be exporting the final grades and exporting the course summary, they should be done in parallel. After exporting the final grades, you need to recalculate the grades. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then one or more of the following paths should be executed: getting approval signatures by the course director, submitting results to SSO. Once submitting results to SSO occurs, you need to send examiners grade approval sheet. The process is now completed.
