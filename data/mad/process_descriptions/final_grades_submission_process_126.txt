The text below is about the final grades submission process. It starts with unmuting all gradebook columns. When unmuting all gradebook columns is completed, one or more of the following paths should be executed: calculating the grades, selecting exam assignment. Then, one or more of the following paths should be executed: applying any changes, reviewing grades summary. Afterwards, the next tasks should be exporting the course summary and exporting the final grades, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: getting approval signatures by the course director and sending examiners grade approval sheet. The process is now completed.
