The following description is about the employee onboarding process. It starts with making an introduction. When making an introduction is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: preparing employee's workstation and preparing benefits package. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: assigning a mentor and touring the office. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then getting approval should be done. After getting approval, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: explaining long-term goals and reviewing onboarding process over the next 60 days. The process is now completed.
