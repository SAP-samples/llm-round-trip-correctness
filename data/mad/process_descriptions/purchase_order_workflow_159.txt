The following description is about the purchase order workflow. It starts with creating the purchase requisition. After creating the purchase requisition, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: getting approval from finance team and getting approval from manager. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the next tasks should be creating the purchase order and informing the supplier the order is ready for approval, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then getting approval from supplier should be done. When getting approval from supplier is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: filing the purchase order and confirming the delivery date. Once filing the purchase order occurs, the next tasks should be sending details to the department and recording the purchase order, they should be done in parallel. The process is now completed.
