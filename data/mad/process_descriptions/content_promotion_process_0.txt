The following description is about the content promotion process. It starts with preparing a summary of your content. After preparing a summary of your content, the next tasks should be commenting on related blog posts and emailing your subscribers, they should be done in parallel. Once commenting on related blog posts occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: submitting to link roundups, reversing engineer your competition. When submitting to link roundups is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: repurposing your content and posting on Q&A sites. Once posting on Q&A sites occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: paying for promotion, performing outreach. The process is now completed.
