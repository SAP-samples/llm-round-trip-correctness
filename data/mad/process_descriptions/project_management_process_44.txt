The following text is about the project management process. It begins when you make an introduction. Once making an introduction occurs, getting approval should be done. When getting approval is completed, laying out a timeline needs to be done. Once laying out a timeline occurs, you need to have a kickoff meeting. Once having a kickoff meeting occurs, recording KPIs needs to be done. After recording KPIs, evaluating project outcomes needs to be done. The process is now completed.
