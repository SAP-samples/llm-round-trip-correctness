The following description is about the content promotion process. It starts with preparing a summary of your content. Once preparing a summary of your content occurs, the next tasks should be paying for promotion, emailing your subscribers and posting on Q&A sites, they should be done in parallel. When paying for promotion is completed, the next tasks should be posting on related forums and groups, repurposing your content and reversing engineer your competition, they should be done in parallel. After repurposing your content, you need to deep-link to the content from older posts. After posting on Q&A sites, performing outreach needs to be done. The process is now completed.
