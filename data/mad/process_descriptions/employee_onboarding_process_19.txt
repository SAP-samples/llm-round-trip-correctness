The following text is about the employee onboarding process. It begins when you make an introduction. When making an introduction is completed, providing a job description with responsibilities needs to be done. After providing a job description with responsibilities, touring the office needs to be done. Once touring the office occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: getting approval, explaining expectations for the following month, assigning first project. After that, one or more of the following paths should be executed: providing reading material for personal growth, encouraging them to socially interact with the team. Once encouraging them to socially interact with the team occurs, one or more of the following paths should be executed: explaining long-term goals, reviewing onboarding process over the next 60 days. The process is now completed.
