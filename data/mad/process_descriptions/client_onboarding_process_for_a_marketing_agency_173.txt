The text below is about the client onboarding process for a marketing agency. It begins when you confirm the received marketing and media resources. When confirming the received marketing and media resources is completed, the process is split into 3 parallel branches: updating client's CRM, getting approval for the first draft and scheduling future calls. When scheduling future calls is completed, the next tasks should be confirming questionnaire is complete and researching the campaign ideas, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: assigning work to the team and informing client of project start. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: sending a post-onboarding survey and making changes based on follow-up call. The process is now completed.
