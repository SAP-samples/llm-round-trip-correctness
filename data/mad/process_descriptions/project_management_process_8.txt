The following text is about the project management process. It starts with making an introduction. Once making an introduction occurs, identifying the approval status should be taken into account. if all approved, then nothing needs to be done, otherwise you should get approval. Afterwards, the next tasks should be laying out a timeline and stating the available resources, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then having a kickoff meeting needs to be done. When having a kickoff meeting is completed, the process is split into 2 parallel branches: keeping communication and recording KPIs. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then one or more of the following paths should be executed: terminating contractors, evaluating project outcomes. The process is now completed.
