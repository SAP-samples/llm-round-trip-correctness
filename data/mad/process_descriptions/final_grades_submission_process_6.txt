The text below is about the final grades submission process. It begins when you ensure 'EX' or zero marks are applied as need. When ensuring 'EX' or zero marks are applied as need is completed, importing gradebook needs to be done. Once importing gradebook occurs, applying any changes should be done. After applying any changes, exporting the final grades needs to be done. Once exporting the final grades occurs, sending examiners grade approval sheet should be done. The process is now completed.
