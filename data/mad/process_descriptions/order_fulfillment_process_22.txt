The following text is about the order fulfillment process. It starts with recording order details. Once recording order details occurs, recording which products were taken from storage needs to be done. Once recording which products were taken from storage occurs, the next tasks should be printing invoice and attaching the shipping label to the packaging, they should be done in parallel. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then the process is split into 2 parallel branches: examining your other orders for delivery overlaps and loading items into the delivery vehicle. Once all pre-tasks are executed, then sending a confirmation email to the customer should be done. The process is now completed.
